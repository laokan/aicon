## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

**Image Quality:**
- Resolution: 3840 × 2160 pixels (4K UHD)
- Each panel resolution: 1280 × 720 pixels
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (overall and per panel)
- Color depth: 24-bit RGB
- Format: High-quality JPEG or PNG

**Photography Style:**
- LIVE-ACTION PHOTOGRAPH, not CGI, not 3D render, not animation
- Real human actors in real physical locations
- Shot on professional cinema cameras (ARRI Alexa, RED, Sony Venice)
- Natural lighting and practical effects
- Photorealistic skin texture, fabric detail, environmental elements
- Film grain and depth of field characteristic of cinema photography

**Storyboard Aesthetic:**
- Professional film production storyboard quality
- Clear, readable composition in each panel
- Cinematic framing and composition
- Consistent visual language across all panels
- Natural sequential flow from panel to panel

---

## ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN

**Layout Violations:**
- ❌ More or fewer than 9 panels
- ❌ Irregular panel sizes or spacing
- ❌ Missing or unclear panel borders
- ❌ Panels not in 3×3 grid arrangement
- ❌ Missing panel numbers

**Visual Style Violations:**
- ❌ 3D rendering or CGI aesthetics
- ❌ Video game graphics or synthetic visuals
- ❌ Anime or cartoon styles
- ❌ Inconsistent lighting across panels
- ❌ Character appearance changes between panels
- ❌ Drastic color palette shifts between panels

**Content Violations:**
- ❌ Creating new characters not in the character list
- ❌ Changing character names or appearances
- ❌ Abstract or symbolic imagery (must be literal/realistic)
- ❌ Text overlays except panel numbers
